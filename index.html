About Us

Coordinating Emergency Medical Services

Seven Mountains EMS Council is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization contracted with the Pennsylvania Department of Health to coordinate EMS programs within our nine-county region.

📍

Regional Coordination

Serving as the liaison between PA Department of Health and regional EMS agencies across 9 counties in Central Pennsylvania.

📚

Training & Education

American Heart Association Community Training Center offering CPR, First Aid, BLS certification courses and EMS instructor training.

🛡️

Resource Management

Equipment coordination, ambulance licensure, receiving facility accreditation, and treatment protocol management.

🎯

Quality Assurance

Data collection, quality improvement programs, complaint investigation, and mass casualty preparedness coordination.

Our Services

Comprehensive EMS Support

Full-service coordination and support for emergency medical services providers, agencies, and the communities they serve.

🚑

911 Dispatch Coordination

Coordinating emergency medical dispatch services and communication systems across all regional agencies.

🎓

EMS Education

Comprehensive training programs including EMT courses, continuing education, and instructor certification.

🔧

Equipment & Resources

Coordinating equipment resources, supplies, and interoperability across agencies in the region.

📋

Strategic Planning

Long-term EMS planning, mass casualty preparation, and regional emergency response coordination.

✅

Quality Assurance

Protocol development, compliance monitoring, and continuous quality improvement programs.

🤝

Inter-Agency Cooperation

Fostering collaboration between EMS agencies, hospitals, and public safety organizations.
